oh-my-multica Delivery Control Architecture

Agent-planned work, Harness-bounded execution, and Loop-controlled delivery.

Harness Engineering

Guides constrain every action before execution. Sensors turn results into reproducible facts and independent quality judgments.

Loop Engineering

The deterministic controller owns delivery state, gates, dispatch, bounded rework, recovery, and stop conditions.

Agent boundary

Agents plan and execute dynamically inside explicit contracts. They cannot redefine evidence or declare the overall delivery complete.
